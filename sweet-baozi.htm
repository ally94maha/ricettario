custard baozi

Ingredients

  • 300g All-purpose flour
  • 4g yeast
  • 55g sugar
  • 4g Baking powder (optional)
  • 140ml 40C warm water
  • 55g oil

  • 112g unsalted butter
  • 2 salted egg yolks
  • 55g custard powder (use Cornstarch instead)
  • 120g powdered sugar
  • 55g whole milk powder
  • 25g milk

Method

  1. Put the unsalted butter into a bowl and melt over a small fire over water.
  2. Put the salted egg yolks in a steamer and steam them, crush them for later use.
  3. Take a cooking bowl, pour custard powder, powdered sugar and whole milk powder, then add melted butter and mix well.
  4. Add the milk in multiple times, then add the crushed salted egg yolk, stir evenly, sieve, and freeze for 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  5. Divide the frozen fillings into 15g portions, round them and set aside.
  6. Take a small bowl, put yeast and sugar, pour the warm water, stir until it dissolves, and set aside.
  7. Take a cooking bowl, put in flour, baking powder and oil, slowly pour in yeast water several times, stir it, knead it into a smooth dough, wrap it in plastic wrap, and proof it for 10 minute.
  8. Roll the proofed dough into long strips and divide them into 30g portions.
  9. After pressing the dough flat, roll it out with a rolling pin while turning it to form a thin round skin in the middle, wrap it in the filling, and close it down.
  10. Put the quicksand buns in a steamer covered with steamer paper, cover and proof for 30 minutes until fluffy, put them in a boiling water steamer, steam for about 6 minutes on high heat.
